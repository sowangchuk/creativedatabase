About Us



Creative Bhutan is for the creative industry developed by the Royal Government of Bhutan. The system makes it easy to avail of creative services, information, and data on the sector. It contains portfolios of all registered creative artists/sectors in Bhutan which helps in generating comprehensive data on the Creative Industry by streamlining the diverse art forms into one system and fostering opportunities such as creating a platform for creative talents to publicize themselves and their events/services to greater market diversity.

Vision of CIS



“A progressive and sustainable creative industry that fosters creativity and innovation for socio-economic development.”

What We Do

High Performance

For a comprehensive data and information on the CI in Bhutan

Connectivity

To connect creative practitioners with the local and global markets To know the number of people employed in the Sector and connect the CI member with those requiring CI services

Promoting

To promote programs, events, and activities related to the CI To help generate income. To store records efficiently and safely

Unique features of the CIS

The CIS is a unique system with features such as: show and hide personal information by registered artists/sector The public/visitors of the site can register to events without logging in Artists can submit forms for approval before logging in
